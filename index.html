Support

Need help? Check out our frequently asked questions or contact our support team.

How do I set up a training session?

Open the app, tap "New Session," and customize your round length, number of rounds, and break duration. Tap "Start" to begin your session.

Can I record my training sessions?

Yes! Enable recording in the app settings, and the app will automatically record each round of your training session.

How do I adjust the sound volume?

Use the volume slider in the app settings to adjust the notification sounds to your preferred level.
