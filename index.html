Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up a new training session?

To set up a new training session, simply open the app and tap the "+" button. You can then customize your round length, number of rounds, break duration, and other settings to match your training needs.

Can I use the app in the background?

Yes, Roundz continues to run in the background. However, please note that iOS may limit background execution time. For the best experience, we recommend keeping the app in the foreground during your training session.

How do I record my training sessions?

To record your training sessions, enable the recording feature in the app settings. Make sure to grant camera and microphone permissions when prompted. Your recordings will be saved locally on your device.

How do I adjust the sound volume?

You can adjust the sound volume in the app settings. The app will use your device's current volume level, so make sure your device volume is set to an appropriate level.

Troubleshooting

Timer stops in background

If the timer stops when the app is in the background, try these solutions:

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed
  • Check that your device's battery optimization is not restricting the app
  • Keep the app in the foreground for the most reliable experience
Recording issues

If you're experiencing issues with recording:

  • Ensure you've granted camera and microphone permissions
  • Check that you have sufficient storage space on your device
  • Try closing and reopening the app
Sound not playing

If you're not hearing the timer sounds:

  • Check your device's volume settings
  • Ensure your device is not in silent mode
  • Verify that the sound is enabled in the app settings
