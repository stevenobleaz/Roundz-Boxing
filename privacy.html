Privacy Policy for Roundz Boxing

Last updated: May 11, 2025

Introduction

At Roundz Boxing, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Camera Access: When you enable the recording feature, we access your device's camera to record your workout sessions. These recordings are stored locally on your device.
  • Photo Library Access: We request access to your photo library only when you choose to save your workout recordings.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core functionality of recording your workout sessions
  • To save your workout recordings to your device's photo library when requested
  • To improve the app's performance and user experience

Data Storage and Security

All workout recordings are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store any of your recordings on remote servers. Your recordings remain private and under your control.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases
  • Apple's AVFoundation framework for video recording

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your recordings stored in your device's photo library
  • Delete your recordings at any time
  • Disable camera access through your device settings
  • Manage your subscription through your App Store account settings

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
