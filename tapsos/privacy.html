TapSOS Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 6, 2025

1. Introduction

TapSOS ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our emergency notification application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Contact information (name, phone number, email) of emergency contacts you add
  • Your device's location (only when SOS is activated)
  • Your name (if provided)

2.2 Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage statistics
  • Camera access (for flashlight control)
  • Microphone access (for audio playback)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To send emergency notifications to your designated contacts
  • To share your location during emergencies
  • To control the device's flashlight for emergency signaling
  • To play alarm sounds at maximum volume

4. Information Sharing

We only share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With your emergency contacts when you activate the SOS feature
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Emergency contact information is stored locally on your device
  • Location data is only transmitted during active emergencies
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Modify or delete your emergency contacts
  • Disable location sharing
  • Uninstall the app at any time

7. Emergency Contact Rights

Your emergency contacts can:

  • Opt out of receiving messages by replying "N" to any message
  • Request removal as an emergency contact

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
