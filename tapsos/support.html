Emergency Alert App - Help & Documentation

Overview

tapSOS is an iOS app designed to provide quick and reliable emergency alerts to your trusted contacts. With a simple tap, you can send your location and alert your emergency contacts when you need help.

Key Features

Quick SOS Alert

  • One-tap emergency alert system
  • Sends your current location
  • Customizable emergency message
  • Works in background

Contact Management

  • Multiple emergency contacts
  • Phone & email support
  • Consent verification
  • Easy management interface

Location Sharing

  • Real-time location updates
  • GPS coordinates
  • Google Maps integration
  • Privacy controls

Getting Started

Initial Setup

  1. Download and install tapSOS from the App Store
  2. Grant necessary permissions when prompted
  3. Add your emergency contacts
  4. Customize your emergency message

Adding Emergency Contacts

  1. Open the Settings tab
  2. Tap "Add Emergency Contact"
  3. Select a contact or enter details manually
  4. Send consent message to the contact

Sending an Emergency Alert

  1. Open the app
  2. Tap the SOS button
  3. Your contacts will receive:
    • Your current location
    • Google Maps link
    • Your emergency message

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Location Not Sharing

  • Ensure Location Services is enabled
  • Check if "Send Location to Contacts" is enabled in Settings
  • Verify location permissions are granted

Contacts Not Receiving Alerts

  • Verify contact consent status
  • Check contact information accuracy
  • Ensure contact hasn't blocked your number/email

Alarm Not Working

  • Check if "Enable Alarm & Lights" is enabled
  • Ensure device volume is not muted
  • Verify Do Not Disturb is not active

Privacy & Security

  • Location is only shared when you tap the SOS button
  • Emergency contacts must give consent
  • Location sharing can be disabled anytime
  • All data is stored locally on your device
