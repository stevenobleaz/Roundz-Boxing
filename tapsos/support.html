# tapSOS - Emergency Alert App ## Overview tapSOS is an iOS app designed to provide quick and reliable emergency alerts to your trusted contacts. With a simple tap, you can send your location and alert your emergency contacts when you need help. ## Features ### Quick SOS Alert - One-tap emergency alert system - Sends your current location to emergency contacts - Includes customizable emergency message - Works even when the app is in the background ### Contact Management - Add multiple emergency contacts - Support for phone numbers and email addresses - Contact consent verification system - Easy contact management interface ### Location Sharing - Real-time location sharing with emergency contacts - Precise GPS coordinates - Google Maps link for easy navigation - Option to enable/disable location sharing ### Alarm & Lights - Loud alarm sound to attract attention - Screen flash for visual alert - Customizable alarm settings - Automatic volume control ## Required Permissions The app requires the following permissions to function properly: 1. **Location Services** - Required for sharing your location with emergency contacts - Used to provide accurate GPS coordinates during emergencies 2. **Contacts** - Required to add and manage emergency contacts - Used to send emergency alerts to your trusted contacts 3. **Camera & Microphone** - Required for video recording feature - Used to capture and share video evidence if needed ## How to Use 1. **Initial Setup** - Grant necessary permissions when prompted - Add your emergency contacts - Customize your emergency message 2. **Adding Emergency Contacts** - Go to Settings - Tap "Add Emergency Contact" - Select a contact from your phone book or enter details manually - Send consent message to the contact 3. **Sending an Emergency Alert** - Open the app - Tap the SOS button - Your emergency contacts will receive: - Your current location - A Google Maps link - Your emergency message ## Troubleshooting ### Common Issues 1. **Location Not Sharing** - Ensure Location Services is enabled - Check if "Send Location to Contacts" is enabled in Settings - Verify that you have granted location permissions 2. **Contacts Not Receiving Alerts** - Verify that the contact has given consent - Check if the contact's phone number/email is correct - Ensure the contact has not blocked your number/email 3. **Alarm Not Working** - Check if "Enable Alarm & Lights" is enabled in Settings - Ensure your device's volume is not muted - Verify that your device's Do Not Disturb is not active ### Privacy & Security - Your location is only shared when you tap the SOS button - Emergency contacts must give consent before receiving alerts -
